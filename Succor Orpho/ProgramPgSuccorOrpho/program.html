Food Donation Program

Take a bite out of Hunger

Tackling hunger on the frontline of emergencies is critical, but we also need long-lasting solutions. That's where we start. When all you know is hunger it becomes all you know of life. It hollows out your body, weakening your immune system and leading to disease.Most of us are fortunate—we don’t know what it’s like to be that hungry.But lots of orphan children do.
"Poverty is a complex issue, but feeding a child isn't" - Jeff Bridges

We want to make a difference .. even if it’s only one meal at a time. But we need your help. That's why we work to help orphanage children to get sufficient food they need to survive and thrive. Simply Choose to Donate Below and also consider joining our mailing list using the form below.

Clothes for Orphan Program

It's better to donate your clothes than to accumulate them unnecessarily

It breaks our heart to say that orphans are deprived of the basic necessities that support daily care. One of these necessities is clothing. Imagine not having anything appropriate to wear in harsh weather conditions… how would you feel?
Orphans go through this misery every day of every year. Even today some orphan children wearing little to no clothing are found. Many of these unfortunate children don’t have clothes to keep them warm in cold weather.

Educational Help Program

A child without education is like a bird without wings.So help orphans to become educationally strong.

By donating to orphans’ education, you can offer them a way out of the neglect, abuse, and exploitation they face when left alone on the streets. It gives them a chance to escape their unfortunate living conditions by equipping them with the right skills and knowledge to build a life that is full of promise and potential.
