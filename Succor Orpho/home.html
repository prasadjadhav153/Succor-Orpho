Rakhi Stall by children

Rakhi is a sacred thread embellished with sister's love and affection for her brother. On the day of Raksha Bandhan, sisters tie rakhi on the wrists of their brothers and express their love to them. In Indian tradition, the frangible thread of rakhi is considered even stronger than an iron chain as it strongly binds a brother and a sister in the circumference of mutual love and trust. This year children from orphanage decided to celebrate Raksha Bandhan in different way.Instead of buying rakhis,they made beautiful and attractive handmade rakhis which depicted their creativity.

Art Activities

Art is not what you see,but what you make others to see.

Art is an effective way of refreshing our mind.The children's mind from orphanage is full of creative artistic ideas.They always love to express themselves through their art.They paint,draw sketches and create beautiful art piece.Often once in a month we plan small outdoor picnics to nearby lakes and hills.Children transform the beautiful lakeside view into an attractive painting.Orphanage organizes art and craft competitions for inspiring the childrens skills.Later on an amazing exhibition is also kept for showcasing their amazing art pieces.

Academic Activities Conducted in orphanage

Education is the key to unlock the golden door of freedom.

Poor or orphaned children are unable to get a chance to learn. They often drop out of school to help provide for themselves or their family, even at an early age. Without an education, the children will be subject to a life of extreme poverty and, at times, may be forced into bonded labor.Thus it is very important task to make them academically strong.Children from orphanage are very intelligent and always eager to learn new things.Various activities are carried out on regular basis.Such as scholarships with cash prizes.We host special guest sessions for children to learn new technology related stuffs.They learn to use computers which is a need in todays world. We connect with various volunteers who wish to teach children free of cost and thus arrange lectures for them.In this way we proceed with such small steps to make their future bright.

Sport Activities Conducted in orphanage

Education is the key to unlock the golden door of freedom.

Physical fitness plays an important role in every child's life.Sports help children to indulge various qualities in themselves such as resilience, leadership, accountability, respect and patience. Along with it it keeps them physically fit leading a healthy life.Orphanage children are very enthusiastic about sports.They love playing different sports such as cricket football,badminton,volleyball,kabaddi and many more.Along with it they also enjoy playing indoor games such as carrom and chess.Proper ground and required equipments are also available for children to play.Competitions are held for various sports.Winners are awarded with prizes and medals.Famous Sport personalities are invited for motivating children to play for national competitions.

Dance and music

Music in the soul can be heard by the universe !

Music can transform the emotions and feelings of the people within no time.It lessen the stress,pain,struggle,distraction and bring positivity and calmness in our daily life.Music holds the power to bring people together. The orphanage childrens posses many music and dance skills.Playing guitar ,piano,violin,drums and presenting different classical dance forms such as bharatnatyam,Kathak and western dance forms such as hiphop is the most hapilly enjoyed moments for them. We arrange competitions and events in which they present their dance and music skills.We also mmotivate them to build their career in dance and music field who wish to do so.

Tree Plantation Program

He who plants a Tree, plant a HOPE !

Tree Plantation is significant task because it is linked to our need for goodfood to eat and clean air to breathe.Twice in a year we organize Tree Plantation Program.Children are guided for how to plant a sapling properly.The aim of the program is to plant a total of 500 saplings in nearby local areas.To promote the tree plantation program and to inspire others to do the same,children make beautiful posters which effectively pass the message to society.
