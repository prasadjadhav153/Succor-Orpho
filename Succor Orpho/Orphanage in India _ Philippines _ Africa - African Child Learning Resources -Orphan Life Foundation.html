Each day, an estimated 734 children
become orphans

Orphan Life Foundation is an international non-profit organization geared towards achieving one ultimate goal: Improving the lives of orphaned children. By providing education opportunities, health and wellness support, foster home and shelter services, and facilitating adoption processes for homeless and parentless children, Orphan Life Foundation focuses on making the lives of orphans and abandoned children better in all aspects.

In this vein, we’ve established numerous orphanages, baby adoption centers and schools with adequate child learning resources across the underprivileged areas of India, Africa and Philippines. Help us make their living conditions better by donating to our cause. Connect with us for more details.

Meet Teressa

Teressa is 10 years old. She lives in one of our orphanages in the Village of Didyr. When we first found Teressa, she had been banished by her community. Her mother had died from HIV and due to the prevalent stigma associated with the disease in her community, Teressa was never accepted as a part of their society.
